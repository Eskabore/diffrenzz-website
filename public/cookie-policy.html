1. Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help:

  • Maintain secure user sessions
  • Remember preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic patterns
  • Improve service quality

2. Cookie Categories

Essential Cookies (Always Active)
  • Purpose: Core functionality and security
  • Example: Session authentication
  • Control: Cannot be disabled
Performance Cookies
  • Purpose: Analytics via Google Analytics
  • Data Collected: Anonymized usage statistics
  • Retention: Up to 26 months
  • Example Cookies: _ga (2 years), _gid (24 hours)
Preference Cookies
  • Purpose: Remember consent choices
  • Example: cookieyes-consent
  • Retention: 1 year
